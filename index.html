Blood Donation Eligibility Criteria

  • *Must be between 18-65 years old.
  • *Weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • *Have a healthy hemoglobin level.
  • *Be in general good health and feeling well.
  • *No recent surgeries or infections.
  • *No tattoos or piercings in the last 6 months.
